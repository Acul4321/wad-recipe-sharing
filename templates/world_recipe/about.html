About World Recipes

Cook delicious dishes easily and reliably.

On each recipe, you can select the number of required portions from 2 to 30, and the site will calculate the right amount of ingredients you need.

You can filter meal types like breakfast, dinner, lunch and dessert . Also, browse by the most popular recipes, most recent, or by country cuisine like Indian, British using our interactive map!.

Please Register & Use all our facilities totally for Free.

Save your favorite recipes by clicking the Favorite button. Access them anytime in your Favorites section.

Want to share your own recipe? Use our easy Recipe Upload feature to add your delicious creations. You can either make them public to share with everyone or keep them private in your Favorites list.

Make a Comment

At the bottom right of each recipe, you'll find a green Comment button. Here, you can leave a comment about the recipe. Comments will be displayed at the bottom of the recipe page.

The author of the recipe will need to approve comments before they are published. The author can also reply to your comments, and the latest comments will appear at the top.

Ingredient Measurements

We recommend converting all ingredients into gram measurements wherever possible for accuracy and consistency. Using a digital kitchen scale can help ensure precise measurements, making your cooking experience smoother.
